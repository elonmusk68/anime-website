Crunchyroll is an on-demand streaming service primarily focused on Japanese animation. It is available worldwide and has the largest streaming catalog of licensed anime. Currently, it has 80 million registered uses and 3 million paid subscribers. What sets Crunchyroll apart from the competition is that you can enjoy most of its content for free. You can stream your favorite anime shows and movies on your PC, phone, or TV just by signing up for a free account. Crunchyroll works on a wide variety of other platforms and devices too, including Xbox, PlayStation, and more.
